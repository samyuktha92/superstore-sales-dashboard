# 🛒 Superstore Sales Dashboard — Power BI

## 📊 Project Overview
Built a professional 4-page interactive Power BI dashboard 
analysing $12.78M in retail sales data across 4 regions, 
3 categories and 793 customers from 2014–2017.

## 🎯 Dashboard Pages
- **Page 1 — Sales Overview:** KPI cards, Sales trend, Regional map, Category analysis
- **Page 2 — Product Performance:** Top 10 products, Bottom 5 by profit, ABC classification, Discount vs Profit
- **Page 3 — Customer Analysis:** RFM segmentation, Top 10 customers, Segment trends, State-wise map
- **Page 4 — Executive Summary:** One-page summary with bookmark navigation

## 🔢 Key Insights
- Total Sales: $12.78M | Total Profit: $1.57M
- Profit Margin: 12.29%
- Technology drives highest revenue
- Consumer segment = 50%+ of total sales
- High discounts directly cause profit loss

## 🛠️ Tools & Techniques
- **Power BI** — Dashboard design & visualisation
- **DAX** — 18+ measures (YoY, MoM, YTD, RFM, ABC)
- **Power Query** — Data cleaning & transformation
- **Bookmarks** — Page navigation panel
